Who We Are

Welcome to "Code Venom," the dynamic coding club at IET DSMNRU! We’re dedicated to sharpening your coding skills, sparking technological innovation, and bridging the gap between classroom learning and real-world applications. With the university’s backing, we offer mentorship, hands-on projects, and industry exposure. Join us and code the future, one project at a time!

Objectives

  • Level Up Your Skills: Hands-on workshops and projects.
  • Join a Thriving Community: Connect and grow with coders.
  • Unlock Opportunities: Access premium resources and guidance.
  • Fuel Innovation: Build projects and compete in challenges.

Be kind, work with integrity, and foster a supportive community. We celebrate diversity and ethical tech practices.
